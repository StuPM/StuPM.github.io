Why lvl N?

May 2, 2016

In Maths, N is always used as a way to represent any possible number.

As a way to keep pushing myself, I’ve incorporated this into my website name and my way of thinking. There is always another level for you to reach, be it in work, school or gaming, you can always learn more and do better.

Always a bigger number, Always something else to learn, Always something else to do, Always something else to see, Always another level to reach in life.
